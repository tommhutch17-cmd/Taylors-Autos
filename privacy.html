Privacy policy

Information we collect

When you request a booking, Taylor's Autos may collect your name, mobile number, email address, vehicle details, registration, requested service and notes about the vehicle.

How it is used

The information is used to manage your booking, contact you about your vehicle, provide repair updates and maintain workshop records.

Who can access it

Authorised Taylor's Autos staff may access booking records. Customer tracking only returns limited job information when the tracking number and mobile number match.

Retention and requests

Contact Taylor's Autos on 01745 350621 to ask about your information, request correction or discuss deletion where appropriate.

Data processor

Booking data is stored using Supabase. Website hosting is provided through Netlify.

This starter policy should be checked and updated by the business before the website is formally launched.
